SnapScan: QR & Barcode — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SnapScan: QR & Barcode ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application.

---

1. Information We Collect

SnapScan does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. Specifically:

  • We do not require account registration
  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact details
  • QR codes and barcodes you scan are stored locally on your device only
  • We do not transmit scan history to any server

2. Camera Access

SnapScan requires access to your device camera solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes and barcodes. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted, stored on external servers, or shared with third parties.

3. Photo Library Access

SnapScan may request access to your photo library to allow scanning of QR codes from existing images. Photos are accessed only when you explicitly choose to scan from your gallery and are never stored or transmitted.

4. Advertising

SnapScan uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Coarse location (country/region level)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by SnapScan. For more information on how Google uses data from apps that use AdMob, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:
Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

5. Local Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted:

  • Scan history (QR codes and barcodes you have scanned)
  • QR codes you have created
  • App preferences and settings

You can delete this data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

6. Children's Privacy

SnapScan is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Third-Party Services

SnapScan uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services — for app distribution. Privacy Policy

8. Data Security

Since SnapScan does not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal security risk. All scan data remains on your device and is protected by your device's own security measures.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
